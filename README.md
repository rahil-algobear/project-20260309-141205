# Echoes of Flora and Tide

A collection of procedural shader art exploring the serene interplay between plant life and oceanic depths.

## Gallery Overview

This collection immerses viewers in the serene interplay between plant life and oceanic depths, crafting a meditative experience that soothes the mind. Each piece invites exploration into organic forms and fluid motions, creating a visual sanctuary that resonates with tranquility.

## Pieces

### 1. Coral Reverie
**Technique:** Raymarching with SDF blending  
**Description:** A vibrant underwater scene where abstract coral formations pulsate gently, creating a sense of depth and harmony. The coral structures morph and sway like living organisms, reacting to an unseen current. Moving the mouse causes the coral to ripple, revealing hidden textures and colors.

### 2. Fractal Ferns
**Technique:** Fractal with FBM noise  
**Description:** A mesmerizing display of intricate fern-like fractals that spiral and weave into a lush tapestry of green. The ferns gently unfurl and curl, simulating the ebb and flow of ocean waves. They dynamically adjust their colors based on mouse position, enhancing their organic feel.

### 3. Luminous Tide Pools
**Technique:** FBM noise with flow field  
**Description:** A tranquil scene depicting glowing tide pools filled with abstract marine life, reflecting a serene night sky. The pools ripple softly, creating fluid reflections that shift with the viewer's perspective. Hovering over the pools causes bioluminescent effects, illuminating the scene with vibrant colors.

### 4. Waves of Geometry
**Technique:** Truchet tiling with kaleidoscope  
**Description:** A geometric interpretation of ocean waves, where triangular shapes create a hypnotic dance of form and light. The shapes shift and rotate, mimicking the dynamic nature of waves in motion. Mouse movement distorts the tiles, creating an interactive wave pattern that reacts in real time.

### 5. Oceanic Whispers
**Technique:** Flow field with Perlin landscape  
**Description:** An ethereal blend of flowing patterns reminiscent of seaweed swaying with the tides, enveloping the viewer in its soft embrace. The patterns ripple and flow continuously, creating a serene, meditative atmosphere. The flow field responds to mouse movement, with a gentle wave effect that enhances the organic feel.

## How to Run

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
3. Click on any piece to view it fullscreen
4. Move your mouse to interact with each shader
5. Press the fullscreen button for an immersive experience

## Technical Details

- **No dependencies:** Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
- **WebGL shaders:** All visuals are procedurally generated in real-time
- **Responsive design:** Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Interactive:** Mouse/touch input affects each piece uniquely

## Browser Compatibility

Requires a browser with WebGL support (WebGL 2.0 preferred, falls back to WebGL 1.0).
